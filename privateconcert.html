Private Concert

Let your true voice free

If you love singing but are so worried about looking bad that you'll never share your voice to public sites like Youtube and Spotify, then check out Private Concert. Private Concert allows you to share your voice with the world - Anonymously.

Sing an entire song or hum a simple melody. It only takes a few taps to record, upload, and share your voice.

Vote on your favorite songs and voices. All your favorite songs are goruped together for you to listen to later.

The entire system is compeltly anonymous. No one knows who you are. No need to worry about embarrassing yourself or stagefright anymore.

The app is built on a smart platform that relies on the cloud. All songs are uploaded and streamed using Parse. No need to worry about storage space!
